About Kyes Guys Pizza

Bringing wood-fired excellence to the Chippewa Valley

Our Story

Kyes Guys Pizza was born from a passion for authentic, wood-fired pizza and a love for bringing people together. We saw an opportunity to bring the pizzeria experience directly to the community—at festivals, private events, and gatherings throughout the Chippewa Valley.

Our mobile setup allows you to watch your pizza being made fresh, right before your eyes. There's something special about seeing the dough stretched, the toppings added, and then watching it emerge from our wood-fired oven with that perfect char and bubbling cheese.

What We Do

Event Catering

From intimate gatherings to large festivals, we bring our mobile wood-fired oven to cook fresh, personal-sized pizzas for your guests. Each pizza is made to order, ensuring everyone gets exactly what they want.

Private Parties

Birthday parties, graduation celebrations, family reunions—whatever the occasion, we'll make it memorable with delicious wood-fired pizza that your guests will be talking about long after the event ends.

Corporate Events

Looking to treat your team or impress clients? Our unique catering setup adds excitement to any corporate function while delivering consistently delicious food.

Community Events

Catch us at local breweries, parks, and community gatherings throughout the Chippewa Valley, especially during the summer months. Follow us on Facebook to see where we'll be next!
