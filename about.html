About Kyes Guys Pizza

Bringing wood-fired warmth to the Chippewa Valley

Our Story

Kyes Guys Pizza was born from a passion for authentic, wood-fired pizza and a love for bringing people together. We saw an opportunity to bring the pizzeria experience directly to the community—at festivals, private events, and gatherings throughout the Chippewa Valley.

There's something special about watching flames dance in a wood-fired oven, seeing dough transform into something delicious right before your eyes. We wanted to share that magic with our neighbors, our friends, and our community.

What started as a dream has become a labor of love. Every pizza we make carries the warmth of a home kitchen and the quality of an artisan pizzeria.

What We Believe In

Fresh Ingredients

We use quality ingredients that you'd find in your own kitchen—fresh vegetables, quality meats, and hand-stretched dough made with care.

Traditional Methods

Our wood-fired oven isn't just for show—it's the heart of what we do. Real wood fire creates flavors and textures you simply can't replicate.

Community Spirit

We're not just serving pizza—we're building community. Every event is a chance to meet new faces and see old friends.

What We Do

Event Catering

From intimate gatherings to large festivals, we bring our mobile wood-fired oven to cook fresh, personal-sized pizzas for your guests. Each pizza is made to order, ensuring everyone gets exactly what they want.

Private Parties

Birthday parties, graduation celebrations, family reunions—whatever the occasion, we'll make it memorable with delicious wood-fired pizza that your guests will be talking about long after.

Corporate Events

Looking to treat your team or impress clients? Our unique catering setup adds excitement to any corporate function while delivering consistently delicious food.

Community Events

Catch us at local breweries, parks, and community gatherings throughout the Chippewa Valley. Follow us on Facebook to see where we'll be next!
